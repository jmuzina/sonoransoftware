Sonoran CAD

Overview

Sonoran CAD is a state of the art, cross-platform, computer automated dispatching and records management system for gaming communities. Sonoran CAD comes complete with real time connections, in-depth customization, voice commands, in-game integration, Discord WebHook notifications and more!

Record Management

Sonoran CAD’s record management system allows users to easily fill out in-depth reports for arrests, citations, warrants, BOLOs and more.

Sonoran CAD allows users to easily search records, licenses and vehicle registrations via character name or license plate. Voice commands also aid in faster searches with audio alerts for warning flags.

Intuitive Search

Sonoran CAD allows users to easily search records, licenses and vehicle registrations via character name or license plate

Voice commands also aid in faster searches with audio alerts for warning flags.

Custom 10-Codes

Customize your community’s 10-codes and penal codes to be easily searched and referenced at any time.

Discord Webhooks

Our Discord integration allows your community to receive real-time Discord WebHooks when:

  • Dispatch calls are generated
  • Police records are modifed
  • Administrative actions occur
  • and much more!

Database Sync

Database Sync allows Sonoran CAD to pull characters, licenses and vehicle registrations directly from your community’s SQL database.
